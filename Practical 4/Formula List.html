Mathematics Formulas

  1. Area of Square = side 2
  2. Area of Circle = 3.14 × (radius) 2
  3. Area of Rectangle = Length × Width
  4. Area of Triangle = 0.5 × base × height
  5. Perimeter of Square = 4 × side
  6. Circumference of Circle = 2 × 3.14 × Radius
  7. Perimeter of Rectangle = 2 × (Length + Width)
  8. Area of Parallelogram = base × height
  9. Surface Area of Cube = 6 × Side 2
  10. Volume of Cube = side 3

Chemistry Formulas

  1. Water = H2O
  2. Carbon Dioxide = CO2
  3. Oxygen Gas = O2
  4. Hydrogen Gas = H2
  5. Sodium Chloride = NaCl
  6. Ammonia = NH3
  7. Sulphuric Acid = H2SO4
  8. Calcium Carbonate = CaCO3
  9. Glucose = C6H12O6
  10. Formation of Water = 2H2 + O2 → 2H2O

Physics Formulas

  1. Speed = Distance / Time
  2. Distance = Speed × Time
  3. Time = Distance / Speed
  4. Force = Mass × Acceleration
  5. Work = Force × Distance
  6. Power = Work / Time
  7. Density = Mass / Volume
  8. Pressure = Force / Area
  9. Kinetic Energy = (1/2) × m × v2
  10. Potential Energy = m × g × h
