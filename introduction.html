D.Y. Patil College of Engineering And Technology, Kolhapur

About Our College

D.Y. Patil College of Engineering and Technology, Kolhapur is a well-known engineering institution providing quality technical education. The college is affiliated with Shivaji University and approved by AICTE.

The college offers various undergraduate engineering programs. It focuses on academic excellence, discipline, innovation, and overall development of students.

The campus has good infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, experienced faculty members, and a positive learning environment.
